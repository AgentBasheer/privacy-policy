Privacy Policy for DarkBrowser

Last Updated: August 31, 2025

Introduction

DarkBrowser ("the App") is a private incognito browser application designed to provide users with a secure and private browsing experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our App.

By using DarkBrowser, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

DarkBrowser is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect any personally identifiable information from our users.

1.1. Information You Provide

The App does not require you to create an account or provide personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

1.2. Information Collected Automatically

DarkBrowser does not automatically collect any information about you or your device. The App does not track your browsing activity, search queries, or visited websites.

1.3. Local Storage

The App stores certain settings and preferences locally on your device to enhance your user experience. This information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers. This information includes:

  • Theme preferences (light/dark mode)
  • Privacy settings (such as download clearing preferences)
  • Tab management settings
  • Favorites and bookmarks (stored locally on your device)
  • Extension preferences

1.4. Analytics and Usage Data

DarkBrowser uses OneSignal for push notifications and Firebase for analytics and configuration purposes. These services may collect limited anonymous data to help us improve the App, including:

  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • App usage statistics (how frequently you use certain features)
  • Crash reports and performance data

Please note that this data is anonymized and cannot be used to personally identify you.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited, non-personal information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionality.
  • To improve and optimize the App's performance.
  • To understand how users interact with the App to guide future development.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues.
  • To send you push notifications about important updates (optional).

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

DarkBrowser does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not share any personally identifiable information because we do not collect any.

We may share aggregated, anonymized data with third parties for research or business purposes. This data does not contain any personally identifiable information.

3.1. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • OneSignal: Used for push notifications. Please review OneSignal's privacy policy at https://onesignal.com/privacy_policy.
  • Firebase: Used for analytics and remote configuration. Please review Firebase's privacy policy at https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

4. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

DarkBrowser offers in-app purchases and subscriptions that are intended to support the ongoing development and maintenance of the App. These purchases include one-time donations (support_coffee_tier, buy_me_lunch, support_dinner_tier) and subscription options (prem_monthly, prem_yearly).

Important Information About Purchases

  • All purchases are final and non-refundable.
  • Purchases are for support purposes only and do not unlock additional features.
  • The App provides the same functionality to all users, regardless of whether they make a purchase.
  • Purchases are processed through the Google Play Store and are subject to Google's terms of service.
  • You can manage your subscriptions through your Google Play account settings.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously. While no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, we implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the information we collect. Since DarkBrowser is designed as a private browser, we do not store any of your browsing data on our servers. All browsing activity remains on your device.

6. Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data since we do not collect any. Local preferences and settings are stored on your device and are deleted when you uninstall the App.

7. Your Rights

As a user of DarkBrowser, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can access the information stored locally on your device through the App's settings.
  • Right to Deletion: You can delete all locally stored information by clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App.
  • Right to Object: You can opt out of analytics collection through your device settings.

8. Children's Privacy

DarkBrowser does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We recommend that you review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

10. Your Consent

By using DarkBrowser, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
